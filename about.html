About Us

MTU Startup Society is a student-run organization at Munster Technological University that supports student entrepreneurship and innovation. The society provides resources, events, and networking opportunities to help members develop their skills and bring their ideas to life.

MTU Startup Student Society is organizing a variety of events to support the growth and development of its members, including trips to startup and business-related events such as the Irish Startup Conference in Trinity College, Dublin, and guest speaker sessions. The society also organizes business competitions, such as the Student Business Hackathon 2023, and networking events for connecting students with industry professionals and other entrepreneurial resources.
