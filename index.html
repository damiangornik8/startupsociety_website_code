Student Business Hackathon 2023

Build your business in 1 day!

A one-day event where students from MTU, UCC and other third-level education institutions will work in teams on their business ideas. In the end, they will present their business ideas to the jury.
Students from all fields can participate! From business to engineering!
During the event, there will be multiple on-site workshops and mentoring sessions with business professionals to help participants develop their business concepts.
Food and drinks are provided.
